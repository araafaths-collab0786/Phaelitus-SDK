# 🏢 NeuralOps Adopters & Sovereign Deployments

This document lists organizations, core platform teams, and sovereign departments that rely on NeuralOps to monitor, detect, and mitigate machine learning data drift and performance anomalies in production environments.

## 🌎 Pinned Enterprise Case Studies

### 💳 Global Retail Corp (Primary Tenant)
- **Use Case:** Continuous evaluation and drift-mitigation for real-time transactional credit scoring and purchase fraud embeddings.
- **Environment:** 3 Federated GKE Autopilot clusters, streaming over high-volume Kafka brokers (>150k msgs/sec).
- **Outcome:** Reduced manual retraining response times from 3 days down to an average of **12 minutes** using zero-touch automated Argo canaries.

### 🏛️ Sovereign Federal Risk Management Node
- **Use Case:** Sovereign compliance auditing, bias evaluation, and continuous continuous control monitoring for state benefits allocations.
- **Environment:** Fully air-gapped on-premise OpenShift nodes synced over a secure Cloud SQL private-IP PostgreSQL cluster.
- **Outcome:** Complete alignment with **NIST AI RMF guidelines** and EU AI Act Article 10 continuous validation protocols.
